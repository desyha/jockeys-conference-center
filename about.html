Our Story

Jockey’s Conference Center (JCC) holds a significant reputation as a renowned conference center situated in Jakarta, Indonesia. Since its inception in 1974, it has played host to numerous important national and international conferences, exhibitions, fairs, indoor sports events, and musical concerts. Notably, it has been the venue for prominent conferences like the 11th Non-Aligned Movement Conference and the Asian-African Conference. These gatherings provided platforms for global leaders, diplomats, and experts to discuss and address crucial political, economic, and social issues.

One of the key factors behind the JCC's success is state-of-the-art infrastructure. Equipped with modern facilities, the center offers a comfortable and technologically advanced environment for various events. Its professional staff members are well-trained and experienced in managing conferences and exhibitions, ensuring that every event held at the JCC runs smoothly. Moreover, the JCC's event spaces are highly versatile, accommodating events of different scales and formats. This flexibility has made it a preferred choice for a wide range of activities.

JCC continues to be an iconic destination that facilitates meaningful interactions and engagements. It serves as a catalyst for global collaboration, hosting international conferences that bring together experts, professionals, and enthusiasts from various fields as it provides a platform for exhibitions that showcase the latest innovations and advancements across different industries.

Certification & Awards

CSEP (Certified Special Events Professional)

Awarded by the International Live Events Association (ILEA), the CSEP certification is designed for professionals involved in the planning and execution of special events. It demonstrates expertise in areas such as event design, logistics, and vendor management.

Accessibility Excellence Award

This award celebrates conference centers that prioritize accessibility and inclusivity for all individuals. It considers factors such as wheelchair accessibility, accommodation for people with disabilities, assistive technologies, and overall inclusivity measures.
